# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 12, 2026

TFT Ninja ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website [https://tft.ninja](https://tft.ninja) (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our Site.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information through Google Tag Manager and similar technologies, including:

- **Usage data:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URLs, and navigation paths
- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and language preferences
- **Network information:** IP address and approximate geographic location

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (via Google Tag Manager) to analyze site traffic and understand how visitors use our Site. These technologies may collect information about your browsing activity across websites.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our Site.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Analyze site usage and trends to improve our content
- Monitor and maintain the performance of our Site
- Understand which guides and content are most useful to our visitors

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics:** Used for site analytics and traffic measurement. Google's privacy policy can be found at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties beyond what is described above.

## Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to the default retention policies of our third-party analytics providers.

## Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
